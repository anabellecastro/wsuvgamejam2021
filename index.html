Huli: A 2D Puzzle-Platformer Adventure

Explore Their Enchanted World

When a mysterious force threatens the harmony of their world, Huli and Maomi rush to drive the darkness away. Inspired by the charm of indie games and the engaging structure of puzzle platformers, Huli follows the aftermath of Huli and Maomi’s momentous sacrifice to preserve the sanctity of their world. Using the last of Maomi's power, Huli enters a series of forgotten places to recover the scattered fragments of their fallen companion and restore balance to the spirit world.

Discover Forgotten Places

  • Explore a mystical world and triumph over the invading dark forces.
  • Traverse landscapes and recover Maomi’s spirit orbs.
  • Restore balance to the spirit world by reuniting Huli and Maomi.
  • Unlock hidden secrets.
